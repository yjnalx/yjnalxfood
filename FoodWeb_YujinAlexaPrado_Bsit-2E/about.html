Holy Week is a time of reflection, repentance, and renewal, and sharing a special meal with family and friends can deepen our connection to these sacred themes. By incorporating traditional and symbolic foods into our dinners, we can create a meaningful culinary experience that enhances our spiritual journey during this holiest of weeks.
